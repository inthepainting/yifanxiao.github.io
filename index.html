Based New York, NY
Program MFA Film, NYU Tisch School of the Arts
Languages English, Mandarin Chinese
Practice Writer-Director, Producer

Yifan Xiao is a writer-director whose work moves between the intimate and the inherited — stories about what we carry from the people who raised us, and the silences that shape us as much as any spoken word.

Their films are driven by restraint and close observation: a cassette tape kept for decades, a melody hummed without knowing, a photograph examined in the wrong kind of light. Working in both English and Mandarin, Yifan explores what lives between cultural identities — not as conflict, but as texture.

Currently an MFA candidate at NYU Tisch School of the Arts, Yifan is developing several projects that center mother-child relationships, memory, and the experience of living between worlds.
