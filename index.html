Yifan Xiao is a Director of Photography based in New York, currently completing an MFA in Film at NYU Tisch School of the Arts. Their work spans narrative fiction, documentary, and experimental forms — with a particular fluency in shooting on film.

Drawn to images that feel held rather than captured, Yifan approaches cinematography as a practice of attention: light as emotional register, frame as point of view. They shoot in both English and Mandarin-language productions, bringing sensitivity to the visual grammar of in-between spaces.

Recent projects include work shot on Kodak 250D and 500T stocks, color graded in DaVinci Resolve using the Kodak 2383 D65 LUT.
